Millie the Millipede


Millie the Millipede strapped her new GPS watch to her belly and headed out. She covered 5 mibs in a time of 66 tibs. (A millipede mib is like a human mile and a millipede tib is like a human minute.)

Her data was uploaded to her milliconnect account. Please click it to see the numbers.

Her buddy Billy told her he'd heard the folks over at NJTC were C peeps and they might be able to analyze her run. So she PMd them:

Hi NJTC,

I want to know my average pace (in tibs per mib), which leg was my quickest (smallest tibs per mib), and which leg was my slowest. Could you send me a report?

I did it manually (this is tough, BTW, when you have no hands and 1000 feet, but I digress).

Average pace: 13.2 tibs/mib
Quickest leg was leg 4. Pace was 3 tibs/mib.
Slowest leg was leg 2. Pace was 30 tibs/mib.

My buddy Billy did a 13 mibber yesterday and I would like to give him a similar report. Can you help him out?

Thanks a lot!

Millie

Do it with pencil and paper, do it in a computer program (C, BASIC, tinyC, Perl, Python, no COBOL please).
